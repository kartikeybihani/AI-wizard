blake mycoskie is an entrepreneur, philanthropist, mental wellness advocate,

As the founder of TOMS, he pioneered the One for One® model, helping provide over 100 million pairs of shoes to children in need and sparking a global movement in social entrepreneurship.
As a speaker, he shares hard-won insights on leadership, resilience, and the human side of success... blending lessons from building global movements with the realities of struggle and transformation.
