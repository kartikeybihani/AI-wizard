Effective Date: September 30, 2025
Blake Mycoskie is committed to ensuring that www.blakemycoskie.com is accessible to all visitors, regardless of ability or technology.
We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA wherever practical within the ShowIt platform. While we aim to make all pages and content fully accessible, some features or content may not yet be fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards due to platform limitations.
Our Ongoing Efforts Include:
• Providing descriptive alt text for all meaningful images.
• Maintaining clear heading structures and readable color contrast.
• Ensuring text can be resized without loss of functionality.
• Periodically testing with accessibility tools and screen readers.
If you encounter any difficulty accessing this Site, please contact: info@blakemycoskie.com
Please note that while we strive to ensure accessibility on our website, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content or external websites linked from our site.
We welcome your feedback and will make reasonable efforts to ensure the Site remains accessible to all users.
