In Waves & War | Now on Netflix
December 2, 2025
I’m deeply honored to share that this is my first time serving as an Executive Producer, and to be part of a project that shines such an important light on the realities our veterans face.
Jon Shenk and Bonni Cohen’s IN WAVES AND WAR follows soldiers returning from Afghanistan whose struggles with traumatic brain injury and PTSD make reintegration nearly impossible. Faced with the heartbreaking rate of veteran suicide, they ultimately discover unexpected hope and healing through a treatment program only legal outside the U.S.
IN WAVES AND WAR opens in theaters October 3 in Los Angeles and begins streaming on Netflix November 3. – Blake
