Blake starts as a mission-led entrepreneur who turns a simple observation into a global model: children without shoes become the trigger for one-for-one. The early identity is builder + storyteller + operator, with purpose baked directly into product design.

## Executive Arc
Blake's arc is not just entrepreneur-to-advocate. It is founder success, public influence, internal collapse, spiritual and emotional recovery, and then reinvention around mental wellness. The most durable theme across eras is this: business is a vehicle, but identity cannot be outsourced to business outcomes.
